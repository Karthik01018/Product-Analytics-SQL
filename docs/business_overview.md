# Business Overview

## Company Background

InsightFlow is a fictional AI-powered productivity platform designed to help individuals and teams manage work more efficiently.

The platform combines task management, note-taking, document generation, team collaboration, and AI-assisted workflows in a single workspace.

This project simulates the type of analytical work a Data Analyst might perform within a growing SaaS company.

---

## Business Objectives

The company aims to:

* Acquire new users through multiple marketing channels.
* Increase user engagement across core product features.
* Improve free trial to paid subscription conversion.
* Reduce customer churn.
* Increase recurring subscription revenue.
* Drive adoption of AI-powered features.

---

## Product Features

InsightFlow offers the following features:

* Task Manager
* Notes
* AI Assistant
* Document Generator
* Team Workspace
* File Sharing

---

## Subscription Plans

### Free

Basic access with limited functionality.

### Pro

Designed for individual professionals.

### Team

Designed for small and medium-sized teams.

### Enterprise

Designed for large organizations with advanced collaboration needs.

---

## Key Business Questions

The analytics team wants to answer questions such as:

### User Growth

* Which acquisition channels bring the most users?
* Which countries show the highest growth?

### User Engagement

* Which features are used most frequently?
* How active are users on a daily, weekly, and monthly basis?

### Trial Conversion

* What percentage of trial users become paying customers?
* Which user segments convert best?

### Revenue

* Which subscription plans generate the highest revenue?
* How is recurring revenue trending over time?

### Retention & Churn

* Which users remain active after signup?
* Which customer segments are most likely to churn?

### AI Feature Adoption

* How many users engage with AI-powered features?
* Does AI usage improve retention and conversion rates?
