# KPI Definitions

This document defines the key metrics used throughout the InsightFlow Analytics project.

---

# User Growth Metrics

## Total Users

Total number of registered users on the platform.

### Business Purpose

Measures overall platform adoption.

---

## New Users

Number of users who signed up during a specific period.

### Business Purpose

Tracks user acquisition performance.

---

## User Growth Rate

Percentage increase in users over a given period.

### Business Purpose

Measures how quickly the platform is growing.

---

# User Engagement Metrics

## Daily Active Users (DAU)

Number of unique users active on a given day.

### Business Purpose

Measures daily engagement.

---

## Weekly Active Users (WAU)

Number of unique users active during a week.

### Business Purpose

Measures short-term user engagement.

---

## Monthly Active Users (MAU)

Number of unique users active during a month.

### Business Purpose

Measures long-term user engagement.

---

## Average Session Duration

Average time users spend in the platform during a session.

### Business Purpose

Indicates user engagement and product value.

---

# Trial Conversion Metrics

## Trial Starts

Number of users who start a free trial.

### Business Purpose

Measures interest in premium offerings.

---

## Trial Conversion Rate

Percentage of trial users who become paying customers.

### Business Purpose

Measures effectiveness of the conversion funnel.

---

## Average Days to Convert

Average number of days required for a user to convert from trial to paid.

### Business Purpose

Identifies conversion speed.

---

# Revenue Metrics

## Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)

Total recurring revenue generated each month.

### Business Purpose

Tracks monthly business performance.

---

## Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)

Projected annual recurring revenue.

### Business Purpose

Measures long-term revenue potential.

---

## Revenue by Plan

Revenue contribution from each subscription plan.

### Business Purpose

Identifies the most valuable plans.

---

# Retention Metrics

## Day-1 Retention

Percentage of users who return one day after signup.

### Business Purpose

Measures initial product adoption.

---

## Day-7 Retention

Percentage of users who return within seven days.

### Business Purpose

Measures early engagement.

---

## Day-30 Retention

Percentage of users who remain active after thirty days.

### Business Purpose

Measures long-term product value.

---

# Churn Metrics

## Churn Rate

Percentage of customers who cancel subscriptions.

### Business Purpose

Measures customer loss.

---

## Churn by Plan

Customer churn segmented by subscription plan.

### Business Purpose

Identifies plans with retention challenges.

---

# AI Feature Adoption Metrics

## AI Usage Rate

Percentage of users who interact with AI-powered features.

### Business Purpose

Measures adoption of AI functionality.

---

## Average AI Interactions

Average number of AI-related actions performed by a user.

### Business Purpose

Measures feature engagement.

---

## AI Usage by Subscription Plan

AI adoption segmented by plan type.

### Business Purpose

Identifies how premium users leverage AI features.
